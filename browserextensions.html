Privacy Browser Extensions

There are thousands of browser extensions available, many of which are designed as useful tools to enhance your browsing experience. They are typically available in popular web browsers addon sections, such as the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, and Microsoft Edge Add-ons.
